Project Description:

This visual interactive dashboard contains analysis of houses sold withing May 2014-May 2015 in King County of Washington. The dashboard contains sheets to 
view average daily house price, a map with pricing details, house prices distribution, a heatmap comparing the view and condition of the house, and distribution of both bedrooms and bathrooms.
The results can be filtered by Month, day, year the house was built, SQft of the house and SQft of the lot.

Technologies used:
Excel
Tableau
